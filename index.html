Expanding Universe Simulation

Add named galaxies to a one-dimensional bungee universe or a two-dimensional space universe. Then stretch space and measure how distances change.

Universe Type

1D mode: galaxies snap onto the black bungee line.

Add Galaxies

Click the bungee line or the black space circle after pressing “Place Selected Galaxy.”

Stretch Space

Stretch makes the universe larger. Galaxies stay attached to their part of space, so distances increase.

Measure Distance

Add at least two galaxies to measure.
